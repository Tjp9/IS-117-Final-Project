What You Will Need

A Workspace: When building a computer it is important to have a large workspace in order to have enough room to build and put together your computer.

Screwdrivers: When building your own computer you will need a phillips #2 screwdriver for most of the computer building process.

A Case: It's important to have a reliable and well made case to put all your components insides of.

Your Components: Clearly when building you pc your will need the components that go inside of your pc. To learn more about the components that go into a pc visit the Components tab.

Tips and Tricks

When building...
  • Dont jam anything
  • Know most components are only made to go in a certain way
  • Try and keep cables neat
  • Stay come when installing components
  • Know that many components click to tell you they are in the correct place
